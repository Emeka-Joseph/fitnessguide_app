Congratulations on Your Decision to Pursue a Healthier, More Active & Energized Lifestyle

We will do everything we can to ensure that you feel welcome, comfortable and healthy. Our mission is to create the best physical fitness evaluation experience possible so that you look forward to coming in to see and recommend us to others. This means extraordinary customer service, functional physical fitness evaluation facilities and fitness professionals, who are not only experts in their field, but truly caring people too.

You may be asking yourself, with so many services available, where do I begin? The Fitness Assessment decision is a great start. We are dedicated to providing appropriate recommendations by reviewing your health and physical fitness questionnaire and performing various assessments and measurements. The recommendations will be based on your individual needs to help you successfully reach your short and long term fitness goals. Some individuals prefer to have a programme being design for them to achieve success; some prefer group fitness or participate in sport programs, while others prefer to try it on their own. We would make sure you get started in the right direction in order not to over exercise or under exercise.

We love having open communication with our clients! Feel free to contact us with any suggestions or concerns. Rest assured, we appreciate your comments and will use your feedback to enhance our services for the betterment of all. We look forward to servicing you along your journey to enriched health and well- being.

Your Fitness

The Fitness Guide International® Fitness Evaluation offers you and your Trainer the opportunity to check your health & wellness status with a comprehensive evaluation of your current fitness levels. Based on our evaluation, your Trainer can gauge your present level of health, and if applicable, the effectiveness of your current training routine. Based on our Fitness Evaluation your Trainer will create an Exercise prescription specific to your present needs.
We will offer suggestions for areas that need improvement as well as guide you to the lifestyle changes you need to experience better health and overall well-being.
